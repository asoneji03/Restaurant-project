Restuarant Description

The Petite Canoli is a fine bakery and cafe that combines the luxeries of Italian and French cusine. We serve fresh coffee and espresso, including a wide variety of lattes, capuccinos, mochas, and other warming drinks such as teas and hot chocolate. Our bakery makes fresh pasteries daily - one for any individual desire! In addition to our sweet menu, we serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We offer a cultured variety of Italian and French favorites. Come and join us for a delactable European treat!
